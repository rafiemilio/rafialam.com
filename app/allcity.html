All-City

1 Player

Welcome to the world of graffiti. Join a crew and make your way across the city leaving your mark while on the path to be All-City.

Uses custom Playstation Move based Spray Can Controller

Controls

Playstation Move and Navigation Controllers
Navigation L-Stick: Move
Navigation L1: Color Wheel
Navigation L2: Paint Mode
X, O: Action/Jump/Exit

Spray Can R-Stick: Select Color
Spray Can Click: Spray/Action
Spray Can Shake: Refill



Keyboard/Mouse Controls
W, A, S, D: Move
Space: Action/Jump/Exit
Return: Start
Left Click: Spray/Action
Scroll: Refill
