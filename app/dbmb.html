Work About

Don't Bite Me Bro!

1-4 Players

Don't Bite Me Bro! is an asymmetrical multiplayer game. Players are tasked with exploring and escaping a monster-infested town. In an attempt to thwart their escape, another player controls the zombies themselves, spawning and herding enemies across the terrain.
